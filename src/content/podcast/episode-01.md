Pre-launch is not a sprint — it's a long game. In this episode, Jane Friedman walks us through the exact framework she recommends for authors who want to walk into launch day with a warm, engaged audience ready to buy.

## Key Takeaways

- Start 6–12 months before publication with a clear positioning statement
- Build your email list as the core asset — social media is rented land
- Identify 5–10 communities where your ideal reader already hangs out
- Create shareable content that solves a real problem for those readers
